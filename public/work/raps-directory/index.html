CAT SANCTUARY DIRECTORY

Helping the community at RAPS Cat Sanctuary identify the over 500 cats in their care

OVERVIEW

RAPS Cat Sanctuary is the largest no-kill shelter of its kind in North America, home to over 500 cats who are cared for by over 100 volunteers. Taking care of that many animals is no small feat in itself, but keeping track of all the different animals and their names can definitely be a challenge for even the most seasoned volunteers.
