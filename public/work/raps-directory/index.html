CAT SANCTUARY DIRECTORY

How do you keep track of hundreds of cats? At RAPS, volunteers and staff care for over 500 felines. I designed a directory to make it easier to identify that many cats.

Client: Richmond Animal Protection Society

My Role: UX Researcher, UX/UI Designer

Timeline: Oct 2025 - present

Tools Used: Figma, Adobe CC, Typeform

THE CHALLENGE

Even the most dedicated volunteers can struggle to remember hundreds of cats

RAPS Cat Sanctuary is the largest no-kill shelter of its kind in North America, home to over 500 cats who are cared for by over 100 volunteers. Taking care of that many animals is no small feat in itself. Remembering individual names, needs, and temperaments is an even bigger challenge.

In order to understand the existing issues and determine if a directory would even be beneficial, I needed to figure out the answer to the following questions:

How do staff and volunteers currently identify cats?

What features make a cat easier to identify?

Are there any specific types of cats that are harder to identify?
