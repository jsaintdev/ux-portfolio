I’m a UX/UI Designer with a technical foundation in full-stack development and a multidisciplinary background in visual art and communications. I’m passionate about creating thoughtful, well-structured digital experiences that help improve people’s lives.

After more than a decade working in entertainment, corporate and service industries, I transitioned into tech through BCIT’s accelerated Computer Systems Technology (CST) program, graduating as Valedictorian in 2025. My design practice blends artistic insight with technical precision, drawing from years of experience in storytelling, traditional painting, and collaborative problem-solving.

My skill set spans the entire UX/UI process — from research and wireframing to component design and front-end implementation. I’ve led event teams, designed digital systems, and built responsive interfaces. I thrive in collaborative environments and bring both empathy and structure to every project.

When I'm not working, I can be found taking care of over 500 cats at the Richmond Cat Sanctuary or baking sourdough pastries from scratch.
